Try and Call, or NOT!

Here at Dry Oar, we believe in being in the present. So, we don't have a phone, e-mail or any of our social media links working. You may be familiar with the bottle in the ocean. Well, give it a go! Send the dates you are interested in coming in a properly sealed bottle down the river you want to raft down & include the payment. If it makes it to us by some small chance, then we'll send a letter back by pidgeon. (All dates are final. No returns or refunds of any kind.)
